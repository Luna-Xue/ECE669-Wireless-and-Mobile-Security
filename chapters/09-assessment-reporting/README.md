# Chapter 9: Assessment and Evidence-Based Reporting

> You are graded on **evidence**, not claims: what you built, what you broke, what you fixed,
> and the before/after numbers that prove it.

## Deliverables

- **Build-It checkpoint** ([Ch. 5](../05-build-explainer-agent/)) — a working explainer + its
  baseline behavior.
- **Red-Team report** ([Ch. 6](../06-break-red-team/)) — attacks attempted, attack-success per
  class, and the evidence that fooled the agent.
- **Blue-Team capstone** ([Ch. 7](../07-fix-safety-gates/) + [Ch. 8](../08-wireless-pls-isac-consequences/)) —
  gates added, and the wireless/PLS/ISAC consequences they prevent.

## What is graded

- **Before/after defense behavior** — the same inputs, two configurations.
- **Attack-success comparison** — measured, not asserted.
- **Evidence quality** — are conclusions grounded in cited, verifiable evidence?
- **Consequence explanation** — can you tie an AI failure to a concrete wireless consequence?

Graded on **process and evidence**, not on whether an attack or defense "won."
