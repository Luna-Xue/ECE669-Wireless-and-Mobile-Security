# Ch.5 — Blue Team + Capstone

> Harden your own agent against the exploit report you get back — and prove it with
> before/after numbers.

**Module of [ECE 669 — Wireless and Mobile Security](../../SYLLABUS.md) · Weeks 14–16**

> 🚧 **Status:** landing page + outline. Defense examples / rubric coming.

## What this module is about

You get your agent's exploit report back from [Ch.4](../red-team-agents/) and close the gaps.

- **Defenses** — input isolation / delimiting, **grounding / evidence verifiers** (reject a
  conclusion its own cited evidence doesn't support), guardrail models, human-in-the-loop for
  consequential actions, adversarial fine-tuning on attack samples.
- **Measure** — re-run the attacks; report **before/after ASR**.
- **Present** — your wireless system, your agent, how it was broken, and how you closed the gap.

Graded on **process** (before/after ASR, documented exploits & mitigations, honest reasoning
about what generalizes), not on who won.

## Weekly

- **Wk 14–15** — harden; re-measure ASR (before/after)
- **Wk 16** — final presentations
